Convert existing system to modern technology?

If the system is written on outdated technologies, it is more difficult to develop and provide security. We will draw up a plan and implement a gradual update of existing software, given the importance of maintaining operability during modernization.

Refresh the look and improve usability

No matter how beautiful the design may be, it should be convenient and understandable to users. We’ll check the usability of your target audience and help you make a product convenient for them. We will develop beautiful and convenient interfaces that attract and retain customers.

Audit the system

We will act as independent experts and evaluate the state of the system, its architecture and infrastructure. We will analyze and research the finished product and offer a list of improvements to make the software as convenient and reliable as possible, including from a security point of view.

Develop an existing system

Any system should be able to scale. We will develop new functionality and make it fit well into the existing system. If new features were not originally intended, we’ll work on a new business logic, architecture, and interface.
